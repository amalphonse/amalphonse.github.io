About Me

Hello, This is Anju Mercian.

After completing my Master's in Computer Engineering from Syracuse University, I worked as a Software Engineer for Intel for half a decade, in their Chandler, Arizona campus. Loved Arizona, such a wonderful state and Chandler was an amazing city; so many places to hike, I love the outdoors. But the summers were really hot, still tolerable. 😀

Moved to the Bay Area for personal reasons, worked freelance as a Technical Project Manager, Business Anaylst and Data Analyst. During my freelancing journey I realized my passion for data; analyzing data and using data for prediction and story telling, which led me to upskilling. I took several online courses in Udacity, Udemy and Coursera on Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Engineering, Full Stack Engineering and Computer Vision, received certifications and upskilled. To gain experience working on real-world projects, I volunteered at a startup social enterprise called Omdena. Currently, I am working full time for Meta as a Data Engineering Consultant.
